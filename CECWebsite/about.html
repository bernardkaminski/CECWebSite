Dean is the Chair of CEC 2016. He is a U3 mechanical engineering student who was born and raised in Montreal. Dean is highly involved in the McGill Engineering Undergraduate Society and been a part of several school initiatives, such as leading committees at McGill's annual Model United Nations conference (McMUN). Apart from his extra-curricular involvement, Dean is also an avid sports fan. He played competitive rugby for five years and rowed for two years. However, he has now retired from competitive sports and has moved on to simpler things. During the summer, Dean can often be found on the golf course, while during the winter on the ski slopes. Now, he is happier than ever to be part the the 2016 Canadian Engineering Competition and can't wait to see how it pans out!
